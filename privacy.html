Privacy Policy

Last updated: 01 April 2026

Ocean Ear ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights — in plain English.

1. Who We Are

Ocean Ear is developed and operated by Sebastian Voss, based in Wellington, New Zealand. For privacy matters, contact us at: hello@oceanear.co.nz

2. What We Collect

We only collect information you actively provide to us. We do not run advertising trackers or sell your data.

  • Beta Signup Form: First name, last name, email address, role, and use case description — collected via Formspree to send you TestFlight access and product updates.
  • Kit Waitlist: Same form as above. Used to notify you when the hardware kit is available for purchase.
  • App Usage (TestFlight): Apple may collect crash reports and usage diagnostics via TestFlight as per Apple's Privacy Policy. We do not receive personally identifiable data from Apple diagnostics.
  • Audio Recordings: All recordings made in the app are stored locally on your device only. We have no access to your recordings.
  • Location Data: If you enable GPS tagging for recordings, location data is stored locally on your device alongside the recording file. It is not transmitted to us.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To send you TestFlight beta access invitations
  • To notify you of product updates and the hardware kit launch
  • To understand who is interested in Ocean Ear (aggregated, not individual profiling)

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any automated decision-making.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Formspree (Privacy Policy): Processes form submissions and forwards them to us by email. Your data is stored on Formspree's servers per their policy.
  • GitHub Pages: Hosts this website. GitHub may log IP addresses per GitHub's Privacy Statement.
  • Apple TestFlight: Manages beta app distribution. Governed by Apple's privacy policy.

We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any other tracking or advertising technology.

5. Data Retention

We retain your email address and signup details until you request deletion, or until the product is discontinued. You can request deletion at any time by emailing us.

6. Your Rights (NZ Privacy Act 2020)

Under the New Zealand Privacy Act 2020, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@oceanear.co.nz. We will respond within 20 working days as required by law.

7. Cookies

This website does not use cookies or local storage for tracking purposes. No cookie banner needed.

8. Children's Privacy

Ocean Ear is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes, we will update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of our website or app after changes constitutes acceptance.

10. Contact

Questions? Email us: hello@oceanear.co.nz

This privacy policy was prepared in accordance with the New Zealand Privacy Act 2020.
