How it works

From ocean floor to your pocket

A complete pipeline from acoustic data collection to actionable marine intelligence.

01

Deploy a Sensor

Ruggedized hydrophone loggers designed for long-term deployment in any marine environment. Survives the Wellington coast — it'll survive yours.

02

AI Analyses the Audio

Our on-device and cloud AI identifies Kina grazing patterns, shipping noise, biological signatures, and anomalies automatically.

03

You Get the Insight

The OceanEar iOS app turns complex acoustic data into clear ecological reports. Share findings, set alerts, export raw audio.

04

Track Change Over Time

Every deployment builds a longitudinal dataset. Understand reef recovery, invasion events, and ecosystem shifts over months and years.

Hardware
🎙️

Passive Acoustic Monitor

8kHz–384kHz sampling. Low-power design for months of autonomous deployment. Marine-grade housing. No active signals — just listening.

AI Engine
🧠

OceanEar AI

Trained on NZ reef acoustics. Detects Kina grazing, invertebrate activity, and anthropogenic noise. Runs on-device for real-time analysis.
